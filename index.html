About Me

I’m a data analyst who genuinely enjoys bringing order to chaos. There’s something satisfying about taking raw, messy data and turning it into insights that actually make sense. I work mainly with Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau to break down complex datasets and shape them into dashboards that are clear, interactive, and easy to understand.

What drives me is the process of organizing information, building clean data models, and designing reports that help people make better decisions with confidence. For me, it’s not just about presenting numbers, it’s about creating clarity and making data feel simple, useful, and impactful.
